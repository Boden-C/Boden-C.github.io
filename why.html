Why this Digital Portfolio is Important

While it can be difficult to show your skills, a good way of doing so is using documentation of your past projects. Applying in the competitive field of computer science requires you to show your programming skills, best done with a portfolio of your comprehension and projects in computer science. Employers can now look at your past code and either see your skills, improvements, and adaptability. Therefore, having a place to show all your coding projects is important to show your coding and other skills.
