Why a Digital Portfolio is Important

(This page has little decoration as it is temporary and the final professional digital portfolio would be a reflection of skills, whether they exist or not)

In this age of technology, businesses now look for proof of their skill instead of merely taking your word for it. While it can be difficult to show your skills, a good way of doing so is using documentation of your past projects. Applying in the competitive field of computer science requires you to show your programming skills, best done with a portfolio of your comprehension and projects in computer science. Employers can now look at your past code and either see your skills, improvements, and adaptability. Therefore, having a place to show all your coding projects is important to show your coding and other skills.
